Mexican Rice & Beef Tacos

Description

These beef tacos with bell peppers, onion, and Mexican rice are quick and easy - just what you need after a busy day!!!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 green or red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (5.4 ounce) package mexican rice
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • 8 taco shells

Steps

  1. Melt 1 tablespoon spread in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat and brown ground beef, seasonsed with salt and pepper, if desired; remove and set aside
  2. Melt remaining 1 tablespoon spread in same skillet over mediium-high heat and cook peppers and onions, stirring occasionally, until crisp-tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in water, mexican rice and tomato and bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, 7 minutes or until rice is tender.
  3. Stir in beef; heat through. Spoon into taco shells and server, if desired, with lime wedges. Top, if desired, with you favorite taco toppings such as sour cream, chopped red onion and shredded lettuce!

Double Cheeseburger

Description

Great recipe for your All-American, double cheeseburger!!!

Ingredients

  • 1 hamberger bun, split
  • 1/3 pound ground beef
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 4 processed cheese singles
  • 1 tablespoon thousand island salad dressing
  • 1 slice tomato
  • 1 leaf lettuce
  • 1 slice onion

Steps

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat. Lightly toast both halves of the hamburger bun, cut sides down, 2 to 3 minutes. Set aside.
  2. Separate beef into 2 portions and form each into a thin patty slightly larger than the bun. Lightly salt each patty and cook on one side for 2 to 3 minutes. Flip patties over and immediately place 2 slices of American cheese on each one. Cook until meat has reached desired doneness, 2 to 3 minutes more. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degress F (70 degrees C).
  3. Assemble the double cheeseburger in the following order: bottom bun, dressing, tomato, lettuce, beef patty with cheese, onion, beef patty with cheese, and top bun.
